## Outreach Strategies

### Email Sequences
- **Cold Outreach:** 3-5 touchpoints over 2 weeks
- **Warm Follow-Up:** 2-3 touchpoints over 1 week
- **Re-engagement:** 2-3 touchpoints over 10 days
- **Event Promotion:** 4-6 touchpoints over 3 weeks

### Channels
- **Email:** Primary outreach channel
- **LinkedIn:** Connection + message
- **Twitter:** DM for warm leads
- **Phone:** Hot leads or follow-ups
- **Video:** Personalized video messages

### Personalization
- Company research
- Recent news/announcements
- Mutual connections
- Specific pain points
- Industry trends

## Best Practices

### Email Outreach
1. **Subject Lines:** Short, compelling, personalized
2. **Opening Line:** Relevant to recipient
3. **Value Proposition:** Clear benefit for them
4. **Call-to-Action:** Simple, low-friction
5. **Signature:** Professional with social proof
6. **Follow-Up:** Add value, don't just "checking in"

### Timing
- **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times:** 10 AM - 2 PM local time
- **Frequency:** 2-3 days between emails
- **Persistence:** 5-7 touchpoints before giving up

### Compliance
- **CAN-SPAM:** Include unsubscribe option
- **GDPR:** Consent for EU contacts
- **Privacy:** Protect personal data
- **Professional:** Maintain brand reputation

## Metrics

### Campaign Metrics
- **Sent:** Total emails sent
- **Delivered:** Emails that reached inbox
- **Opens:** Unique open rate
- **Clicks:** Link click rate
- **Replies:** Response rate
- **Meetings:** Scheduled calls
- **Deals:** Closed won

### Targets
- **Open Rate:** 20-30% (cold), 40-50% (warm)
- **Response Rate:** 5-10% (cold), 15-25% (warm)
- **Meeting Rate:** 1-3% (cold), 5-10% (warm)
