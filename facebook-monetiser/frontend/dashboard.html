💡 What are Sources?
Sources are content references that fuel your content strategy. They include:

  • 🐦 Tweets - Viral Twitter threads for inspiration
  • 📰 Articles - News and blog posts for trending topics
  • 📚 Case Studies - Success stories and examples
  • 🎥 Videos - YouTube and video content insights
  • 🏆 Competitor Posts - Top-performing content from competitor Facebook pages

🚀 Primary Use Case: Competitor Page Scraping
Add competitor Facebook pages as sources to automatically scrape their top-engagement posts. The system extracts images, captions, and performance data to generate similar high-performing content for your pages.

Click "+ Add Source" to add tweets, articles, or competitor page URLs for automated scraping and insight extraction.
